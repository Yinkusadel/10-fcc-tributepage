UZUMAKI NARUTO

The man who saved a million lives

Here is a timeline for Uzumaki naruto's life:

  • 1945 - Born in hidden leaf village,konoha
  • 1945 - Lost his family due to a war in the village the night he was born
  • 1960 - left his village to train with master jiraya
  • 1962- comes back to the village during an attack in the village by the AKATSUKI
  • 1970- ended the shinobi 4th great ninja war
  • 2000- dies at d age of 55
